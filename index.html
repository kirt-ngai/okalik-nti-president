A message to Nunavummiut

The results are in, and they did not fall our way. I called to offer my congratulations, and my full support for the work ahead.

Running for President of Nunavut Tunngavik Inc. has been one of the great honours of my life. Over these weeks I travelled our communities, listened to elders and youth, and heard the same hope again and again: a Nunavut where every Inuk can thrive. That hope does not end with an election.

I am grateful to the volunteers who gave their evenings and weekends, to the families who welcomed me into their homes, and to every person who took the time to vote. Democracy only works when people show up, and Nunavummiut showed up.

The eleven priorities we campaigned on remain the work of our generation: housing, food security, health, our children, jobs, education, infrastructure, climate, our land, safe communities, and Inuit business. I will keep pushing for them, in whatever way I can serve, alongside all of you.

To the next President: you have my hand and my help. Our people are counting on all of us to row in the same direction.

Okalik Eegeesiak
Candidate for President, Nunavut Tunngavik Inc. · 2026
